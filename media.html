Messaging guidance

These rules keep the brand clear, credible and aligned with the evidence boundaries used across the website.

Say

  • Research-origin spin-off emerging from Instituto Superior Tecnico.
  • Human-centered AI for breast imaging workflow support.
  • Designed to support radiologists and clinical teams.
  • Evidence is being developed through publications, pilots and validation activities.

Avoid saying

  • Autonomous diagnosis or radiologist replacement.
  • Regulatory clearance unless separately confirmed.
  • Guaranteed cost savings or patient outcomes.
  • Partner endorsement without written permission.
