# BreastScreening-AI Reports Source Register

Last reviewed: 2026-06-15 14:51:43 WEST (+0100)

## Purpose

This register maps the source materials available for Annual Financial Statements, IR Presentations, IR Letters, and IR News and Notice assets published or planned for `reports.html`.

The register distinguishes primary evidence, supporting business context, public communication material, and records that still require confirmation. Public assets must use only source-backed facts, dates, figures, and events.

## Access Boundary

The files listed below were identified in the locally synchronized Google Drive folders, uploaded materials, and repository. Live Gmail, Google Calendar, Google Drive metadata, and ReadAI records were not directly searchable during this review because the required connectors were unavailable.

Exported Gmail files and locally synchronized Drive documents are valid evidence that a record exists, but final publication decisions should still confirm the latest version, approval status, and document owner.

## Evidence Priority

Use sources in this order:

1. Signed financial statements, signed accounting records, executed agreements, formal grant documents, and approved corporate records.
2. Board or account-approval materials, trial balances, official correspondence, and final program documentation.
3. Approved business plans, investor presentations, roadmaps, financial models, and press releases.
4. Existing public website pages, public PDFs, and repository claims registers.
5. Draft decks, scripts, one-pagers, proposal claims, projections, and inferred narrative.

Lower-priority material must not override a higher-priority source.

## IR Letters

Primary financial basis:

- The applicable annual or interim signed accounting package.

Business and strategy context:

- Package V8 full presentation and business project.
- Package V8 roadmap and P&L model.
- Current approved website evidence and `CLAIMS_REGISTER.md`.
- One-pagers and pitch scripts for historical narrative only.

Funding and activity context:

- Startup Voucher operation documentation and `Sensiperception_Cartaz_VoucherStartups_print.pdf`.
- EIC Pre-Accelerator official documents and grant agreement when available.
- Confirmed clinical pilot records and approved public claims.

Recommended letter structure:

1. What occurred during the reporting period.
2. Why the confirmed developments matter.
3. Financial position and funding dependency.
4. Product, clinical, and evidence progress.
5. Risks, missing information, and management response.
6. Priorities for the next period.

Do not use:

- Unconfirmed payment dates.
- Unapproved partner or customer statements.
- Draft valuations or forecasts.
- Regulatory or clinical deployment claims not supported by primary records.
