# BreastScreening-AI

Welcome to the BreastScreening-AI documentation hub.

BreastScreening-AI is a multidisciplinary initiative exploring how artificial intelligence can support breast cancer screening, clinical decision-making, and healthcare efficiency. The project brings together clinicians, researchers, engineers, and business contributors.

> BreastScreening-AI is a research and decision-support initiative. It does not replace qualified healthcare professionals, clinical judgment, diagnosis, or emergency medical services.

## Project objectives

- Investigate AI-assisted approaches for breast screening and image analysis.
- Support clinicians with interpretable, evidence-informed decision-support tools.
- Reduce avoidable errors and improve consistency in screening workflows.
- Explore interfaces and workflows that fit clinical practice and human oversight.
- Build collaborations between healthcare, research, technology, and innovation partners.

## Contributing

Use the repository's [Issues](https://github.com/BreastScreeningAI/meta/issues) area for public, non-sensitive technical questions, documentation proposals, and reproducible problems. Use [Discussions](https://github.com/BreastScreeningAI/meta/discussions) for broader community and research conversations.

When reporting a problem, include:

- A concise description of the issue.
- The page, repository, or component involved.
- Steps needed to reproduce it.
- Expected and observed behavior.
- Browser, operating system, and relevant version information.

Never include patient data, medical images, passwords, access tokens, or confidential organizational information.
